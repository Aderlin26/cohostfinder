CoHost Hub Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026
Last Updated: January 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to CoHost Hub ("we", "us", "our", or "Company"). CoHost Hub connects property owners with co-hosts for rental management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

Account & Profile Information

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely by our authentication provider; we do not store passwords in plain text)
  • Name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Account type (Property Owner or Co-host)

Co-host Profile (if you create one)

  • Bio
  • Location (a text location you enter, e.g., city/region)
  • Commission percentage
  • Years of experience
  • Services offered
  • Availability
  • Profile photo (optional)

Bookings & Marketplace Activity

  • Property address
  • Booking start/end dates
  • Booking notes
  • Booking status (pending/accepted/declined/completed/cancelled)

Messages & Reviews

  • Messages you send through the App
  • Reviews/ratings you submit

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, our systems may automatically collect limited technical data for security and reliability purposes, such as:

  • IP address
  • Timestamps of requests
  • Basic device/app information needed to operate the service

Note: CoHost Hub does not request your precise device location. Any "location" shown in profiles is the location the user types into their profile.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide the marketplace features (profiles, bookings, messaging, reviews)
  • Facilitate communication between property owners and co-hosts
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Maintain and improve the App

4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

4.1 Sharing With Other Users

Depending on how you use the App, certain information is shared with other users:

  • Co-host profile information (e.g., name, bio, services, location, rating)
  • Booking information needed to coordinate a booking
  • Messages you send to other users
  • Reviews you publish

4.2 Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate the App. For example:

  • Convex (cloud database + backend) to store and process App data.

4.3 Legal / Safety

We may disclose information if we believe it is necessary to comply with law, protect users, or enforce our Terms of Service.

4.4 We Do Not Sell Personal Data

We do not sell your personal information.

5. DATA RETENTION

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide the App. If you delete your account, we delete your account and associated data as described below. Limited information may remain in backups for a short period as part of routine system operations.

6. ACCOUNT DELETION (IN-APP)

CoHost Hub supports account deletion directly inside the App.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the App
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

When you delete your account, we delete your account and associated marketplace data (such as your co-host profile, bookings, messages, reviews, and conversations), subject to any retention required by law.

7. SECURITY

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CoHost Hub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last Updated" date when changes are made.
